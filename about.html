Ari was born in New York, but currently resides in Taipei, Taiwan. He enjoys cycling, regularly competing in Taiwan amateur circuit races. He is also an avid musician, having played in many punk and country-folk bands. 

He is interested in intersection of urban planning and economic justice. He is also interested in design, data visualization and cartography and what their implications for effective research may be.
