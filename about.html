Ari is an urban planner specializing in spatial data analysis and mapping. He is interested in the intersection of planning and economic justice and in the norms that surround contemporary planning tools.

Having worked mainly in spatial analytics, he is interested in data-drive approaches for solving policy-challenges. He is also interested in design, data visualization and cartography and what their implications for effective research and engagement may be.

He was born in upstate New York, has lived in Taipei, Taiwan and currently in New York City. He enjoys cycling, playing music, screen-printing, and riding trains.
