Citibike Trip Orientation

Using CitiBike trip data, I color coded individual trips for September 2017, the record high use month, by the orientation of the trip. The idea is to understand patterns in CitiBike trips, whether they are used against the grain of subway lines, like cross-town in Manhattan or north-south trips in Brooklyn.

Made in QGIS  and Illustrator.
September, 2017.
