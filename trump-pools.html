Pools and Trump in NYC

A tongue-in-cheek web-map purported a correlation between the presence of private swimming pools and the percent vote for Trump in the 2016 Presidential election. The map uses data from the NYC Open Data Portal and city's Board of Elections.

Mapped using Leaflet. Analysis in R. Graphics in Illustrator.

February, 2018.
