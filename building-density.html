Taipei Building Footprint Density

Using building footprint data I produced with the team at Taipei Urban Data Lab, I delineated the density of the buildings in central Taipei. 

The wide streets and superblocks of the central government and new business districts form two large holes in the map.

Made in QGIS  and Illustrator.
October, 2015.
