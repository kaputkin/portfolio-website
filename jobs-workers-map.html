Jobs and Workers

(Work in progress)

A web-map built on Leaflet that maps jobs by sector and where the workers in that sector live in NYC. Using data from the longitudinal employer-household dynamics program, I mapped 2005 and 2015 data which is comparable by moving the slider left and right for a before and after snapshot.

A 10 minute walkshed overlay from subway stops can also be added.

Made with R, Python, QGIS, Leaflet and Tilemill. February 2018.
