Fastest roads in NYC

Using proprietary data from Dash Labs, a company that collects driving data from its userbase, I am able to determine the average speed on road segments where those drivers drove. The map depicts roads that at least three users drove on from October to December 2017.

The chart details the highest average speed on any road segment. Surprisingly, the highest average speeds all occured on 25MPH roads.

Made with R, QGIS and Illustrator.
January, 2018.
